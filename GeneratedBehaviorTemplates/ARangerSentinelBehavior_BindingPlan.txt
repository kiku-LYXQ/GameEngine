Blueprint Binding Plan
----------------------
Spec ID: ranger-sentinel-v1

1. Blueprint Inheritance
   • Create Blueprint BP_ARangerSentinelBehavior that inherits from ACharacter.
   • Keep the generated C++ skeleton intact; Blueprint overrides should call the Execute<Hook>Hook helpers.
   • OptionalBlueprintClass slot is reserved for injecting a variant Blueprint or actor subclass when needed.

2. Resource Slot Guidance
   • StaticMeshSlot (UStaticMesh*): 用于 Blueprint 绑定的 StaticMesh. 绑定提示: /Game/Characters/Ranger/SMesh_RangerBody
   • SkeletalMeshSlot (USkeletalMesh*): 用于 SkeletalMesh 绑定. 绑定提示: /Game/Characters/Ranger/SK_Ranger
   • MontageSlot (UAnimMontage*): AnimationMontage 用于动作切片. 绑定提示: /Game/Characters/Ranger/Montage_RangerAim
   • NiagaraSlot (UNiagaraSystem*): Niagara 特效插槽. 绑定提示: /Game/FX/Ranger/ChargedTrail
   • SoundCueSlot (USoundCue*): 声音提示插槽. 绑定提示: /Game/Audio/Ranger/ShotImpact
   • MaterialSlot (UMaterialInterface*): 用于覆盖材质. 绑定提示: /Game/Materials/M_RangerArmor
   • BlueprintClassSlot (TSubclassOf<AActor>): 可选 Blueprint 子类槽, 绑定提示: /Game/Blueprints/BP_RangerVariant

3. Behavior Hook Execution Outline
   · Blueprint 事件图或行为树节点应调用 Execute<Hook>Hook，确保资源槽在执行前就绪。
   · 资源槽 StaticMeshSlot、SkeletalMeshSlot、AnimationMontage、NiagaraEffect、SoundCue、Material、OptionalBlueprintClass 可在钩子中提供插值素材。
   1) OnSpawn – Initialize perception grid, apply stealth overlay, and report ready.
      · 在 Blueprint Graph 或动画通知中调用 ExecuteOnSpawnHook，并通过上面的资源槽设置视觉/音频/特效。
   2) OnTargetAcquired – Raise bow, align shots, and play charge FX.
      · 在 Blueprint Graph 或动画通知中调用 ExecuteOnTargetAcquiredHook，并通过上面的资源槽设置视觉/音频/特效。
   3) OnRetreat – Play vanish montage, spawn shields, and notify AI director.
      · 在 Blueprint Graph 或动画通知中调用 ExecuteOnRetreatHook，并通过上面的资源槽设置视觉/音频/特效。

4. Component Checklist
   • Required components:
     - CharacterMovementComponent
     - AIControllerComponent
     - HealthComponent
   • Optional components:
     - CoverComponent
     - DialogueComponent

5. Metadata & Routing
   - spec_id: ranger-sentinel-v1
   - domain: npc
   - stability: high

   · 触发 curl http://127.0.0.1:7000/api/copilot/generate 并在日志中确认路径。
